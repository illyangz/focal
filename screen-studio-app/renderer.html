Zoom

Purple blocks on the timeline are zoom moments — click a block to select it (the Zoom level slider then edits just that block), double-click to remove.

Timeline

Tracks: video (cuts appear on it in red) · audio waveform · zoom · camera · overlays · masks. Drag blocks to move, drag edges to resize, double-click to remove, click to edit/select. Darkened ends = trimmed off.

Overlays

Slides fill the frame with text; annotations are draggable labels; images and video clips overlay the recording (screenshots, B-roll, intros). All added at the playhead.

Masking

Masks pixelate sensitive areas and track your zooms. Click a gray block to select a mask, then drag the patch on the preview to place it.

Cursor

The system cursor baked into the capture is erased each frame (a soft blur at its exact logged position), then re-drawn as vector art — sharp at any size, smoothed, fading when idle, and able to glide home for loopable videos.
