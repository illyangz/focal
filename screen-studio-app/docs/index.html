Everything you need, nothing you don't

+

Automatic zoom

Detects your clicks and zooms in smoothly. Every zoom is editable on the timeline.

↺

Smooth cursor

The system cursor is hidden and re-drawn from precise movement data — resized, smoothed, with click ripples.

■

Backgrounds & styling

Gradient or custom backgrounds, padding, rounded corners, shadows. Vertical, square, or widescreen.

◉

Webcam & voice

Draggable camera bubble, full-screen camera moments, and studio-style voice enhancement.

✂

Real editing

Cuts, trims, text slides, annotations, image and B-roll overlays — all on a multi-lane timeline.

▦

Privacy built in

Pixelate sensitive areas with masks that follow your zooms. Everything renders locally on your machine.
